About Us

Shivusha Energies Private Limited (SEPL) is a leading solar energy solutions provider, specializing in land aggregation, project coordination, and EPC services. With a strong commitment to sustainability, we deliver high-quality, cost-effective solar power projects that drive India's transition to clean energy.

At SEPL, we harness the power of the sun to build a brighter, greener future. Our dynamic and innovative approach ensures cutting-edge solar solutions that are environmentally responsible and economically viable.

Our Mission

To be a trusted leader in sustainable energy solutions, empowering communities through high-quality solar projects that promote environmental conservation and energy independence.

Our Vision

To become a renowned name in India's renewable energy sector, recognized for our expertise, innovation, and commitment to excellence.
